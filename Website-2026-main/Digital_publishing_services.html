Accessibility Solutions

Building an Inclusive Digital Experience for All

At PRGEEQ, we are dedicated to ensuring that education, technology, and opportunity are accessible to everyone. Accessibility is not a single feature but a comprehensive framework of inclusive design principles and user-experience best practices that support equal participation for all users—regardless of visual, auditory, cognitive, or mobility challenges.

A Learning Management System (LMS) that aligns with WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) and global accessibility frameworks establishes the foundation for an inclusive learning and development ecosystem. By integrating accessibility into every stage of design and development, organisations can ensure equitable and consistent learning outcomes for all employees.

Understanding Accessibility Standards

    • WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines): A globally recognised standard for creating accessible digital content, also aligned with ISO/IEC 40500:2012.
    • ADA (Americans with Disabilities Act): A civil rights law ensuring equal opportunity for individuals with disabilities.
