The Real Problem: Why Most Alt Text Workflows Fail at Scale

Enterprise decision-makers typically face one of two costly models:

Fully Manual Alt Text Writing:

Challenges include:

  • High per-image costs
  • Slow turnaround for large textbook programs
  • Inconsistent terminology across multiple writers
  • Difficulty scaling during peak production cycles
  • Operational bottlenecks

Manual-only models are accurate — but often financially unsustainable at enterprise volume.

Fully AI-Generated Alt Text

AI can produce captions quickly — but in educational publishing, speed without oversight creates risk.
Common enterprise issues:

  • Misinterpreted math equations and symbolic notation
  • Inconsistent descriptions across similar visuals
  • Context-blind outputs that ignore instructional purpose
  • Over-description of irrelevant details
  • Under-description of key relationships
  • Remediation costs after accessibility audits

AI-only models often reduce initial cost but increase downstream correction and compliance risk.
